ABOUT US

For nearly a century, PraRoz has been an integral part of the heart and soul of Mangalore. Since our inception in 1925, we've been more than just a bakery - we've been a cherished tradition woven into the fabric of our community. From timeless favorites like Benne Butter to the comforting embrace of Cashew Macaroons, we've been honored to play a role in the special moments of your lives.


Our array of 85 distinctive products serves as a bridge between generations, carrying with them the nostalgia and warmth that only time can cultivate. These cherished treats have stood as a testament to our commitment to quality and excellence. With our ISO 22000:2018 Certified production units, we ensure that every creation maintains the highest standards of safety and taste, from our ovens to your table.


In each bite, in every flavor, PraRoz invites you to create new memories while cherishing the old. We're not just about baking; we're about crafting experiences that linger long after the last crumb is savored. As we continue to be a part of your story, we invite you to join us on this delicious journey and become a part of the legacy that is your city, your bakery - Praroz.
